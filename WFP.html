XR Interaction & Metrics Evaluation at PICO

Overview

At PICO, I contributed to research and development in XR interaction design and evaluation, focusing on interaction methods, usability metrics, and system efficiency. My work spanned gesture-based interactions, spatial navigation, and immersive experience optimization, aiming to enhance usability and comfort in extended XR sessions.

Key Contributions

1. XR Interaction Methods & Metrics Analysis

  • Conducted quantitative evaluations of various XR interaction techniques, including raycasting, gesture-based control, and hybrid interaction models.
  • Developed metrics-based frameworks to assess efficiency, fatigue, and accuracy in user interactions.
  • Analyzed motion mapping strategies for different input modalities to refine user experience.

2. Prototype Development for Interaction Testing

  • Built prototype systems to test and benchmark different interaction techniques in VR/MR environments.
  • Designed experiments to measure user response times, error rates, and ease of use.
  • Explored gesture raycasting optimizations to improve precision and responsiveness.

3. Contribution to XR Design & Research

  • Investigated new spatial interaction paradigms to support intuitive and seamless user engagement.
  • Worked on refining context-sensitive menus and gesture-based UI navigation for enhanced user accessibility.
  • Contributed to internal discussions on next-generation XR interaction frameworks.

Impact

  • Helped establish quantitative evaluation methods for emerging XR interaction techniques.
  • Influenced design decisions related to interaction frameworks and usability improvements.
  • Advanced gesture-based and hybrid interaction methods to reduce user fatigue and increase precision.

Technologies & Tools

  • Unity (XR Toolkit, Interaction System)
  • HCI Metrics Analysis & Statistical Evaluation
  • Gesture Recognition & Raycasting Techniques
  • Prototype Development & Experimentation
